A BRIEF INTRODUCTION ABOUT CYES

Coooperation Of Young Entrepreneurs is a brilliant profit making startup. It is the now for the prospect of the future. CYES was birthed by one man on fire which has spread its tentacles thereby involving other cofounders. CYES is an investment company who also aims at discovering and raising young entrepreneurs for global relevance. CYES focuses on investing in agriculture real-estate and technology. Added to this we have the BNM Foundation which seeks to empower the less privilege so they can intend fent for themselves and take care of their communities. The idea within CYES is explosive and is proving its ability to carry on in to a bright future, there is still more about CYES to be known

CYES is a place of opportunities. We will seek to see that every founding member has an equal opportunity and is not disregarded. This goes down to decision making where no vital decision will be made or implemented without the concern of all the founding members. Opportunities will also be given to founding members to represent CYES in other places. It will be expected of our representative to put CYES at heart and keep its standards.
